Description

Kaimati is a famous delicacy found on the coast of Kenya and eating in many other East African Countries. They are crunchy dumplings in a sweet sugar syrup known as kaimati or kalimati.

They make for a delightful snack, enjoyable at any time; as a starter sweet before a meal, for a light dessert with coffee, for breakfast if you have a sweet tooth, with tea in the evening, midnight sweet craving etc etc.
